Full name model

Accuracy: 98%

This model is trained on 6,30,795 bengali names. This model will work best with full bengali names.

The full name model has higher accuracy because its better at identifying the effect of words on entire name. For example, "Islam" is evidently present in both female and male names so its effect on overall gender is less than other words in the name. This model can identify that while the word-wise model cannot.

Word-wise model

Accuracy: 80%

This modelis trained on 21,678 single word names which were extracted from full names and were assigned their gender based on the full name they were extracted from.

The name is divided into individual words and passed to the model which then outputs the detected gender of the word and also how confident it is. The final verdict is calculated adding and subtracting the gender and confidence values. For example, a name with two words where one word is low confidence male and the other is medium confidence female, will be considered female.
